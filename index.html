Data Visualization Final Project

Several visualizations (and discussion) of the question "Where should young people move after graduation?"

Created by Chris Tillotson, Nick Jobe, and Matthew Luisi, Fall 2025

Values Bar Plots

This plot visualizes how young people feel about certain "family values", based on their responses to three different questions.

Home Price Trends

This map visualizes average home prices, including single family homes and apartments / condos, in the 100 most populous cities in the US.

Double click on a state to the right to show only cities in that state, and click more states to enable more.
